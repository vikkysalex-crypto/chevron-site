our lower carbon targets

carbon targets anchor

We are investing to grow our oil and gas business, lower the carbon intensity of our operations and grow new energies businesses. We set targets to lower our carbon emissions intensities by 2028.*

*Targets refer to emissions intensity improvements announced by Chevron.
